Original Cardactors

A collection of characters and modifications deposed upon Balatro by Gwyn (hellgnoll) and June (junipertheory) at the behest of their generous patrons. Jokers, textures, a hodge-podge of changes with maximal compatibility and all sorts of new little friends.

Requires latest Steamodded. Supports Malverk and CardSleeves.
